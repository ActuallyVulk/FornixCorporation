ABOUT FORNIX

Dynamic and expanding, Fornix Corporation specialises in merchandising and distribution of premium consumer goods. From the islands of Masbate and Catanduanes to all trade routes across the Bicol Region, our reach spans all Our operations in the merchandising and distribution areas are committed to perfection. Our extensive distribution system guarantees that premium consumer products satisfy the needs of a varied clientele by reaching every part of the Bicol Region.

FORNIX OBJECTIVES

Fornix Corporation aims to maximise profitability by means of development and maintenance of lucrative accounts and efficient allocation of resources for operations with low cost. By encouraging a culture of leading by example and giving our staff members delegated authority and chances for professional development, we hope to improve leadership. Furthermore, we aim to foster friendship and cooperation, create a suitable environment for development, and be a benefit to the society by presenting job possibilities and proving civic-mindedness.

FORNIX VISION

Dedicated to offer all of our clients complete quality services, we are a fast-growing and highly appreciated distribution firm. Our dedication goes beyond simple transactions; we treat our clients as valued business partners, therefore cultivating close, cooperative relationships based on mutual respect and confidence. Understanding and predicting their demands helps us to regularly surpass expectations, therefore strengthening our profile as a dependable, customer-centric company. Our method is based on the conviction that our success is directly related.

Booking Salesman

Job Description:

Fornix Corporation is looking for a motivated and dynamic Booking Salesman to join our sales team. As a Booking Salesman, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining strong customer relationships, generating sales orders, and ensuring that products are delivered on time to meet customer demand. You will be responsible for driving sales growth and maximizing revenue opportunities in the Bicol Region, which includes the islands of Masbate and Catanduanes.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and maintain a thorough understanding of the company's product portfolio, pricing, and promotions so that you can effectively promote and sell products to customers.
  • Identify and prospect for new customers within assigned territories or market segments in order to grow the company's customer base and sales opportunities.
  • Negotiate sales agreements, terms, and conditions with customers to ensure profitable business transactions and meeting sales targets.
  • Prepare and maintain accurate records of sales activities, customer interactions, and order transactions with sales management software or CRM systems.
  • Provide management with timely and accurate sales reports, forecasts, and insights to help with decision-making and strategic planning.
  • Monitor market trends, competitor actions, and customer preferences to identify sales opportunities, threats, and areas for improvement.


Qualifications:
  • 20 years and above
  • Male or Female
  • College Level or College Graduate
  • Good communications skills
  • With or without experienced in sales
  • Self-driven and result oriented
  • With leadership ability
  • Willing to be trained and be assigned anywhere in Bicol

Interested applicants may apply personally at Fornix Corporation, San Francisco, Guinobatan, Albay near PLT college, or send it to fornix.mcmontalban@gmail.com.

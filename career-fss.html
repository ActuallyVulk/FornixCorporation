Field Sales Supervisor

Job Description:

Fornix Corporation is looking for a dynamic and results-driven Field Sales Supervisor to lead our sales team in Bicol Region. As a Field Sales Supervisor, you will be responsible for leading and motivating a team of sales representatives to meet sales targets, increase market share, and provide excellent customer service. You will be responsible for driving sales growth, implementing sales strategies, and ensuring that field sales activities are carried out effectively.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Lead, coach, and mentor a team of field sales representatives to meet sales targets and objectives in their assigned territories or market segments.
  • Develop and implement sales strategies, tactics, and action plans to boost sales, increase market penetration, and broaden the company's customer base.
  • Conduct regular field visits and ride-alongs with sales reps to offer advice, support, and feedback on sales performance, customer interactions, and territory management.
  • Monitor and analyze sales metrics, KPIs, and performance indicators to identify trends, opportunities, and areas for improvement, and take corrective action as necessary.
  • Collaborate with internal departments such as marketing, product management, and operations to ensure that sales efforts are in line with the overall business goals and objectives.
  • Lead by example and demonstrate best practices in sales techniques, customer service, and relationship-building to inspire and motivate the sales team to achieve excellence.


Qualifications:
  • 25 years and above
  • Male or Female
  • College Graduate of any course
  • Good communications skills
  • With experienced in sales
  • Self-driven and result oriented
  • Self-driven and result oriented
  • Willing to be trained and be assigned anywhere in Bicol

Interested applicants may apply personally at Fornix Corporation, San Francisco, Guinobatan, Albay near PLT college, or send it to fornix.mcmontalban@gmail.com.

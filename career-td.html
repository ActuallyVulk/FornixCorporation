Truck Driver

Job Description:

Fornix Corporation is looking for a dedicated and experienced Truck Driver to join our logistics team. As a Truck Driver, you will be responsible for ensuring the safe and timely delivery of our products to various destinations throughout the Bicol Region, including the islands of Masbate and Catanduanes. You will be in charge of operating company-owned trucks and ensuring compliance with all transportation regulations and company policies.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Safely operate company-owned trucks to transport goods to designated locations within the Bicol Region, including Masbate and Catanduanes.
  • Load and unload products onto trucks using appropriate equipment and techniques to ensure proper cargo handling and security.
  • Plan efficient routes to reduce delivery time and fuel consumption while staying on schedule and meeting customer expectations.
  • Conduct vehicle inspections before and after each trip to ensure roadworthiness and compliance with safety regulations.
  • Maintain accurate records of vehicle inspections, mileage, fuel consumption, and delivery logs.
  • Adhere to all traffic laws, regulations, and company policies to ensure safe and responsible driving practices.
  • Communicate effectively with dispatchers, warehouse staff, and customers to coordinate delivery schedules and address any issues or concerns.
  • Handle any incidents or emergencies on the road professionally and in accordance with company protocols.

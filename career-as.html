Accounting Staff

Job Description:

Fornix Corporation is looking for a motivated, detail-oriented Accounting Staff to join our finance team. As an Accounting Staff, you will be responsible for supporting a variety of accounting functions such as financial reporting, accounts payable/receivable, and general ledger maintenance. You will collaborate closely with other members of the finance department to ensure the accuracy, compliance, and efficiency of our financial operations.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Process accounts payable transactions, such as vendor invoices, expense reports, and disbursement requests, to ensure payment accuracy and timeliness.
  • Perform accounts receivable functions such as invoicing, billing, and collections, as well as reconcile customer accounts, to ensure timely and accurate payment processing.
  • Assist with month-end and year-end close processes by preparing journal entries, reconciling accounts, and ensuring financial data accuracy and completeness.
  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date records in the general ledger system, including the chart of accounts, account reconciliations, and accompanying documentation.
  • Assist in the preparation of financial statements, reports, and analyses to help management make decisions and comply with regulations.
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders to resolve accounting issues, answer questions, and provide financial data as needed.
  • Support external audits by providing requested documentation, reconciliations, and explanations of accounting processes and transactions.

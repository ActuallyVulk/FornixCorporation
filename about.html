ABOUT US

OUR NATURE AND PURPOSE

Fornix Corporation is a dynamic and growing enterprise specializing in the distribution and merchandising of high-quality consumer products. Our reach extends across all trade channels throughout the Bicol Region, including the islands of Masbate and Catanduanes.

Fornix Corporation operates with a commitment to excellence in the distribution and merchandising sector. Our comprehensive distribution network ensures that high-quality consumer products reach every corner of the Bicol Region, addressing the needs of a diverse customer base.

HOW THE BUSINESS CAME ALONG

Today, Fornix Corporation boasts multiple warehouses strategically located across the Bicol region, ensuring efficient and effective distribution of our diverse product offerings.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to assure a fair return on our investment by developing profitable accounts and making wise use of our resources. We strive to exercise leadership by example and delegate authority through empowerment, fostering camaraderie and teamwork based on trust, and providing an environment conducive to growth and development. We aim to be an asset to the community by providing employment opportunities and displaying civic-mindedness. Our commitment and dedication are reflected in our adoption of the inherent Filipino values of "MALASAKIT" and "BAYANIHAN."

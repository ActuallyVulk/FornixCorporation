HR Supervisor

Job Description:

Fornix Corporation is looking for an experienced and dynamic HR Supervisor to oversee our Human Resources department. As an HR Supervisor, you'll be responsible for developing and implementing HR strategies, policies, and programs to attract, retain, and develop top talent. You will be in charge of overseeing all aspects of human resources operations, including recruitment, employee relations, performance management, training and development, and compliance with labor laws and regulations.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and carry out human resources policies, procedures, and programs to support the company's goals and objectives, ensuring compliance with best practices and legal requirements.
  • Oversee the recruitment and selection process, which includes job postings, candidate screening, interviews, and onboarding, in order to attract and retain qualified candidates for various roles.
  • Coordinate employee performance management processes, such as goal setting, performance evaluations, feedback sessions, and development planning, to increase employee engagement and productivity.
  • Provide managers and employees with HR-related advice and support, such as employee relations, conflict resolution, disciplinary actions, and policy interpretation.
  • Conduct investigations into employee complaints, grievances, and misconduct allegations, and make recommendations on appropriate actions to address issues and ensure fair and equitable treatment of employees.
  • Develop and deliver training programs and initiatives to help employees improve their skills, knowledge, and competencies, as well as to support career development and succession planning.
  • Ensure compliance with labor laws, regulations, and company policies by conducting regular audits, monitoring, and enforcement of HR practices and procedures.


Qualifications:
  • Female 21 years and above
  • Preferably a Graduate of BS Psychology and other Business Related Courses
  • Experience in handling HR functions
  • Must be computer literate, keen in details and intelligent
  • Willing to be trained

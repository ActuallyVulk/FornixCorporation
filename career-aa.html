Assistant Auditor

Job Description:

Fornix Corporation is looking for a detail-oriented, analytical Assistant Auditor to join our finance team. As an Assistant Auditor, you will support the audit process by ensuring compliance with internal controls and regulatory requirements. You will collaborate closely with the audit team to examine financial records, assess risks, and provide valuable insights into improving operational efficiency and financial integrity.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist in developing and carrying out internal audit procedures to assess the effectiveness of internal controls and financial processes.
  • Conduct a thorough examination of financial documents, transactions, and records to detect discrepancies, errors, or irregularities.
  • Conduct thorough testing of accounting data and procedures to ensure accuracy and compliance with company policies and regulatory requirements.
  • Stay up-to-date on industry trends, best practices, and regulatory changes that affect auditing and financial reporting.
  • Assist with external audits and regulatory inspections by preparing necessary documentation and facilitating audit inquiries.
  • Participate in special projects and initiatives as directed by management to enhance internal controls, processes, and systems.


Qualifications:
  • Female
  • 21 years and above
  • Candidate must possess at least a Bachelor's/College Degree, Finance/Accountancy or equivalent.
  • Must be computer literate, keen in details, flexible and intelligent
  • Willing to travel
  • Fresh Graduates are also welcome to apply

Interested applicants may apply personally at Fornix Corporation, San Francisco, Guinobatan, Albay near PLT college, or send it to fornix.mcmontalban@gmail.com.
